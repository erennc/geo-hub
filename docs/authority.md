# Content Authority Signals — What AI Engines Trust

> AI search engines prioritize authoritative content. Here's how to signal authority.

## Key Authority Signals

### 1. Statistics & Data
- Include specific numbers with sources
- Link to original studies/reports
- Use recent data (within 1-2 years)

**Example:** "AI Overviews now appear in 52% of tracked searches (Source: BrightEdge, Feb 2025)"

### 2. Expert Quotes & Attribution
- Quote recognized experts in your field
- Include author credentials
- Use proper attribution format

### 3. Citations & References
- Link to authoritative sources (academic papers, industry reports)
- Use inline citations, not just link dumps
- Cite primary sources, not aggregators

### 4. Authorship Signals
- Clear author bylines on all content
- Author bio with credentials
- Link to author profiles (LinkedIn, Twitter)

### 5. Freshness Signals
- "Last updated" dates on all content
- Regular content updates
- Timestamps on data and statistics

## What Reduces Authority

- ❌ Unattributed claims
- ❌ Outdated statistics
- ❌ No author byline
- ❌ Thin content without depth
- ❌ Keyword stuffing
- ❌ Content without external citations
