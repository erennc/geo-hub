# Measuring GEO Success — Metrics & Tools

> You can't improve what you don't measure. Here's how to track your GEO performance.

## Key Metrics

### 1. AI Citation Rate
How often your brand/content is mentioned in AI-generated responses.

**Tools:** Otterly.AI, Peec AI, Knowatoa

### 2. AI Referral Traffic
Traffic from AI platforms to your website.

**How to track:** UTM parameters, referral source analysis in Google Analytics
- Look for referrers: `chat.openai.com`, `perplexity.ai`, `gemini.google.com`

### 3. Brand Mention Sentiment
Whether AI engines describe your brand positively, neutrally, or negatively.

**Tools:** Manual testing, Profound, ZipTie

### 4. Citation Accuracy
Whether AI engines accurately represent your product/brand.

**How to track:** Regular manual queries across platforms

### 5. GEO Audit Score
Your website's technical AI-readiness score.

**Tool:** `geo-audit` CLI tool

## Tracking Cadence

| Metric | Frequency |
|--------|-----------|
| AI Citation Rate | Weekly |
| Referral Traffic | Weekly |
| Brand Sentiment | Monthly |
| Citation Accuracy | Monthly |
| GEO Audit Score | After each site update |

## Getting Started

1. Run `geo-audit` for your baseline score
2. Set up one monitoring tool (Otterly.AI has a free tier)
3. Create a spreadsheet tracking weekly citation counts
4. Test 5-10 relevant queries across ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude
5. Document your baseline, then track improvements monthly
